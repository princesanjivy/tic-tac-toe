Tic Tac Toe is the ultimate game of strategy and fun! Play offline against a challenging AI, compete with a friend on the same device. Enjoy smooth and immersive transition animations for every element of the game, making your gaming experience visually pleasing.

Tic Tac Toe is built on the Flutter framework, which ensures a smooth and responsive user experience.
